# 📘 **Chapter 9.2: Neural Architecture Search (NAS)**

This chapter explores an advanced and exciting idea:

👉 **Can AI design better neural networks automatically?**

---

## 🔹 1. What is Neural Architecture Search?

### Definition

👉 **Neural Architecture Search (NAS)** is a technique where:

* AI automatically designs neural network architectures
* Instead of humans manually choosing layers and parameters

---

### Simple Idea

👉 Instead of asking:

* “How many layers should I use?”

👉 NAS answers:

* “Let the machine decide the best structure”

---

### Analogy

Think of building a house:

* Traditional → architect designs
* NAS → computer tests many designs and picks the best

---

## 🔹 2. Why Do We Need NAS?

Designing neural networks manually is:

* Time-consuming
* Requires expertise
* Trial-and-error

---

👉 NAS helps by:

* Automating design
* Finding better architectures
* Saving effort

---

## 🔹 3. What Does NAS Search?

NAS tries to optimize:

* Number of layers
* Number of neurons
* Type of layers (CNN, RNN, etc.)
* Connections between layers

---

👉 It searches for the **best architecture**

---
