# 📘 **Chapter 8.4: Speech & Audio Processing**

This chapter explores how neural networks work with **sound and speech**:

👉 **Enabling machines to hear, understand, and generate audio**

---

## 🔹 1. What is Speech & Audio Processing?

### Definition

👉 It is the field of AI that allows systems to:

* Recognize speech
* Analyze audio signals
* Generate human-like voice

---

### Simple Idea

👉 Just like humans use ears to hear,
machines use algorithms to process sound

---

### Analogy

Think of a conversation:

* You hear sound
* Convert it to meaning
* Respond

👉 AI systems do the same

---

* Voice typing

---

## 🔸 4.2 Text-to-Speech (TTS)

👉 Convert text → speech

Example:

* Voice assistants

---

## 🔸 4.3 Speaker Recognition

👉 Identify who is speaking

---

## 🔸 4.4 Emotion Detection

👉 Detect tone/emotion from voice

## 🔸 4.5 Audio Classification

## 🔹 6. Typical Pipeline

---

### Step-by-Step

1. Capture audio
2. Convert to waveform
3. Extract features (spectrogram)
4. Feed into model
5. Generate output
