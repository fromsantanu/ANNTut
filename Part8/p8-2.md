# 📘 **Chapter 8.2: Natural Language Processing (NLP)**

This chapter introduces another major application of neural networks:

👉 **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** — enabling machines to understand and work with human language

---

## 🔹 1. What is NLP?

### Definition

👉 **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** is a field of AI that allows computers to:

* Understand human language
* Process text and speech
* Generate meaningful responses

---

### Simple Idea

👉 Just like humans understand language,
NLP helps machines do the same

---

### Analogy

Think of a translator:

* Reads a sentence
* Understands meaning
* Converts into another language

👉 NLP models work similarly

---

## 🔹 2. Why NLP is Important

Language is everywhere:

* Chatbots
* Search engines
* Translation tools
* Voice assistants

---

👉 NLP makes machines **interactive and intelligent**

---

## 🔹 5. Word Representation (Very Important)

---

### 🔸 One-Hot Encoding

* Each word → vector
* Simple but inefficient

---

### 🔸 Word Embeddings

👉 Words represented as dense vectors

---

### Example

* “king” and “queen” → similar vectors

---

👉 Captures meaning

---
