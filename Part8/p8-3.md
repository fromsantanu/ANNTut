# 📘 **Chapter 8.3: Neural Networks in Healthcare**

This chapter focuses on one of the most impactful applications of AI:

👉 **Using neural networks to improve healthcare and save lives**

---

## 🔹 1. Why AI in Healthcare?

Healthcare deals with:

* Large amounts of data
* Complex patterns
* Critical decisions

---

👉 Neural networks help by:

* Detecting diseases early
* Assisting doctors
* Improving treatment planning

---

### Simple Analogy

Think of a doctor:

* Looks at symptoms
* Analyzes reports
* Makes decisions

👉 AI acts as a **smart assistant**, not a replacement

---
